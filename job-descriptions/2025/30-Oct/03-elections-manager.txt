EEO Policy Statement
Personnel Policy 2.01 - "It is the policy of the Kalamazoo County Board of Commissioners to provide equal employment opportunities to qualified persons without regard to race, color, sex, age, religion, national origin, marital status, a person's political affiliation, sexual orientation or gender identity, height, weight, disability, genetic information, or any other legally protected status."
Important:
- Each Sunday, open job postings are updated.
- As indicated, apply separately for each listed position.
- Do not send your resume via email. You must complete an application to be considered for a position.
- A drug test is required for all positions.
- Credit checks and criminal background checks may be required for certain positions.
