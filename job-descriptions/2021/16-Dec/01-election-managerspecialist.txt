Running for Office?
The filing deadline for the May 5th, 2026 Primary Election is fast approaching. Candidate filings must be received in our office no later than 4 PM on Wednesday, February 4th. For more information see our Candidate and Local Issue Information page.
Voting Absentee?
Absentee voters must submit an Absentee Ballot Request Form for each election in which they wish to receive a ballot. You can print that form at home by clicking here, or you may contact us to be sent an application.
