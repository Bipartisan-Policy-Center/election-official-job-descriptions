=== JOB DESCRIPTION ===

We have extended the closing date for this position to January 23rd. If you have already submitted an application, there is no need to reapply.

The Larimer County Clerk & Recorder Elections division offers an outstanding opportunity for a rewarding career in the ever-changing, always engaging field of Election Administration – where the foundation of government begins for our citizens!

Larimer County is increasingly aware of the need to attract and retain the best people for our workforce, and we are seeking skilled Elections Supervisors to join our highly respected team. We serve a population of more than 300,000 citizens, of which more than 250,000 are registered voters. We embrace innovative processes and have a solid reputation for integrity.

If you are a self-motivated, positive team player who thrives in a fast-paced professional environment – we want to hear from you! The successful candidate will be dedicated, assertive, and possess exceptional interpersonal and problem-solving skills. The process of Election Administration is project-driven and very detail-oriented. The position of Elections Supervisor supervises the daily performance of staff and operations related to the conduct of Elections. Elections Supervisors lead either the Voter Operations or Ballot Operations process area, as assigned. 

Specifically, this position:
• Exercises direct, first-level supervision of employees, operations, and activities; may include coordinating staff training, assigning and reviewing work, timesheet approval, participating in the interview selection process, making recommendations impacting pay, status, and disciplinary action, evaluating performance independently or in conjunction with a higher-level manager, and approving time off.
• Evaluates and assists in the development of training goals, objectives, and priorities and provides input concerning implementation.
• Reviews, updates, and refines Election procedures yearly or as required and ensures compliance throughout all Elections process areas.
• Reviews legislative changes impacting Elections processes and proposes process updates.
• Supervises Voter Operations or Ballot Operations process area, as assigned.
Voter Operations tasks:
• Supervises customer-facing support services, such as Point of Service and Voter Registration.
• Provides project management oversight of voter operations services, such as Election Judge recruitment and training, outgoing mail ballot materials, and Voter Service and Polling Centers.
• Oversees the support of intergovernmental agencies such as municipalities and special districts in the conduct of regular and special elections.
• Administers and ensures compliance with the National Voter Registration Act (NVRA).
• Serves as direct contact and project liaison for print/mail vendor and any other applicable equipment vendors.
• Manages appropriate archival procedures and guidelines for Elections materials, including, but not limited to, Special District notices, contracts and invoices.
• Provides accountability for all election-related supply ordering, accounting and invoicing; tracks charges to correct budget; reviews invoices and billing.
Ballot Operations tasks:
• Oversees the communication of ballot access qualifications and requirements to local candidates. Documents and leads the Petition process for Recall, Initiative, Major Party Candidate and Unaffiliated/Minor Party Candidate Petitions.
• Ensures compliance with all Election Legal Notices and Publications required by law.
• Provides accountability for the integrity of Voting System equipment. Complies with all requirements of Voting System Conditions of Use.
• Provides project management oversight for the receipt, processing, verification and tabulation of mail ballots, and all machinery needed for the successful operation of those processes. Assigns, directs and supervises temporary employees to ensure day-to-day operations are carried out efficiently and statutorily. Leads problem solving and daily balancing of processes.
• Serves as direct contact and project liaison for voting system vendor, ballot sorter vendor, ballot extractor vendor, and any other applicable equipment vendors.
