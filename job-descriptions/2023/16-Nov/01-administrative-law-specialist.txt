=== JOB DESCRIPTION ===

This position serves as an Administrative Law Specialist in the Regulatory Section to assist and advise the supervisor, State Elections Director, Department of State Executive Office, Bureau of Elections staff, county and local clerk community and other interested parties on Michigan Election Law, Michigan Campaign Finance Act, Michigan Lobby Registration Act and other legal issues involving the Bureau of Elections. This position will review, draft, and analyze legislation and administrative rules impacting the Bureau of Elections and draft implementation requirements to be provided to staff. The incumbent will also review records for compliance with existing laws and provide necessary changes.
