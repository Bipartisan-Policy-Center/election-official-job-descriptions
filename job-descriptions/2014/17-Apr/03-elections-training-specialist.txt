Board Vacancies
The Secretary of State will post all pending or existing vacancies in the position of Director and/or Deputy Director of a board of elections for public review on the Secretary of State's Web site. The minimum qualifications for directors and deputy directors are available in Chapter 2 of the Ohio Election Official Manual.
The Secretary of State will also post to this page any job vacancy notice voluntarily submitted by boards of elections for full time board employee positions.
